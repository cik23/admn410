Delivery polices:
Edit My Repeat Delivery Schedule

You can edit your Repeat Delivery subscription at any time. Simply sign in to your online account and click on the Repeat Delivery link to update your shipping address, payment method, quantities, delivery frequency, next ship date or delete your subscription. Return policies:

Free Returns or Exchanges to Any Petco or Unleashed by Store

Your local DHP store or Unleashed by DHP store will gladly accept the return of your dhp.com order within 60 days of the initial shipment date.
Before returning merchandise to a Petco or Unleashed by DHP store, print your receipt. Please have your order details handy. If you do not have a receipt, you will be issued a merchandise credit for the listed store price or allowed to exchange the product for something else.
If you received an incorrect item or are requesting a refund of shipping charges due to a petco.com error, pleasecontact Customer Service. Personalized items and prescription food cannot be returned to a store.
Exchanges can be made at any DHP or Unleashed by DHP store location. If exchanging for a different item, you will receive credit for the amount paid as listed on your "Returns to Store Receipt."
Orders placed online using your PayPal account may be returned to any Petco or Unleashed by Petco store for a refund on your credit card or may be exchanged.
For returns made after 60 days, you will be issued a merchandise credit for the paid price or allowed to exchange the product for something else.
Purchases made online using your PayPal account may be returned for a merchandise credit or may be exchanged.
